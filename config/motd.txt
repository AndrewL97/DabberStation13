<b>Controls :</b>
Hold Left Mouse Button - Fire/Look Around
1,2,3,4 - Intents
Shift - Sprint (hold)
Space - Jump (hold to fall slower and jump higher)
Arrow Keys/WASD - Move
Q - Drop Item
X - Swap Hand
Z - Use Item In Hand
O - GLOBAL CHAT
T - LOCAL CHAT
E - Board/Exit vehicle
M - Use vehicle item
